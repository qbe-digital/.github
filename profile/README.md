## Who We Are

**QBE Digital Solutions** is a Saudi technology and creative solutions company helping businesses transform ideas into secure, scalable, and high-performing digital products.

We combine business strategy, product design, software engineering, cloud infrastructure, and digital growth to deliver complete solutions—from initial discovery and planning through launch, support, and continuous improvement.

<br>

> **Quality Beyond Expectation.**  
> We build digital experiences designed to perform, scale, and create lasting business value.

### 💻 Software & Web Development

Custom web applications, corporate websites, SaaS platforms, API integrations, backend systems, performance optimization, and production-ready digital architecture.

### 📱 Mobile App Development

## Product in Development

### Autivy

**Autivy** is an upcoming multi-tenant SaaS platform being developed by QBE for the automotive care industry.

The platform is being engineered to help automotive businesses manage:

`Branches` · `Services` · `Bookings` · `Operations` · `Subscriptions` · `Memberships` · `Branded Digital Experiences`

<br>

![Development Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/STATUS-PRIVATE_DEVELOPMENT-C89B3C?style=for-the-badge)
